We pride ourselves in meeting your needs. Each of our clients come to us with a different expectation and need, we try to deliver that by building a relationship with you and allowing the process to be as hands on or off as you would like. This is what our typical process is like:

1. Website Design Request
This first step allows us to get a better idea of your current situation and how we might be able to help!
2. The Design Process
We allow our clients to be as involved as they would like to be during the design process and tailor the experience to allow as much creativity as you would like.

For example if you consider yourself:

Not Creative
We can show you some different design options and we can tweak the template to your needs.

Somewhat Creative
We can look at all of the different elements and allow you to move them around and play with the content and layout.

Super Creative
We also welcome those who are creative enough to design the website and show us their creation and then we can handle the "tech stuff"
3. The First Draft
From your input we will create a mock up of your site that allows you to see it live before it actually goes live. From there we can make any additional tweaks or changes you would like before the website goes live for your clients!
4. The Final Product
The completed website is completed and can be hosted on a platform of your choosing or we can host the website for you. We even can make monthly updates, build new products, add on to the website and more!

To get started help us understand your current situation and current needs to we can best begin this new process!
